Our Story

MORE THAN A GYM.
A COMMUNITY.

XTREME BODY HEALTH AND FITNESS has been a cornerstone of health and wellness in Rajapalayam for over a decade. What started as a commitment to the community has grown into one of the most trusted fitness centers in Tamil Nadu.

We believe fitness is for everyone — not just athletes. Whether you're recovering from an injury, just starting your journey, or chasing a new personal best, our team meets you where you are.

  • Experienced, certified trainers with deep knowledge
  • Equipment and classes suited to all fitness levels
  • Specialised rehabilitation and physiotherapy services
  • Steam & sauna for full-body recovery
  • Over 10 years serving the Rajapalayam community
